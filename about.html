About MUWA

Discover our mission, vision, and commitment to excellence

Who we are

Machakos University Western Association (MUWA) is an association that incorporates members from Western region of Kenya and those with roots from Western Kenya, who are students at Machakos University (MKsU). It is a social unit that revolves around all that its members take part in.

Vision: To achieve universal standards in service delivery to our members to pursue global opportunities.

Mission: Establish proper communication and interaction systems that enable skill development and through knowledge dissemination uphold core values and integrity for universal benefits.

Objectives

Cultural Preservation

Promote awareness, pride & celebration of Luhya heritage through events, language forums and traditional arts.

Leadership Development

Mentorship, capacity-building workshops and preparing students for governance roles.

Unity & Integrity

Strengthen solidarity, encourage teamwork and champion inclusivity.

Networking

Connect with alumni, faculty and community leaders for mentorship and internships.
