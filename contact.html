MUWA is a student-led cultural and social organization that brings together all students from Western part of Kenya at Machakos University to promote unity, heritage, and personal development.
Any student at Machakos University who identifies with or appreciates Luhya culture is welcome to join.

MUWA organizes a variety of events including cultural festivals, educational workshops, and social gatherings aimed at promoting Luhya culture and fostering community among students.
Absolutely! MUWA events are inclusive and open to all students interested in learning and celebrating Luhya culture.
MUWA is governed by a committee elected by its members. The committee is responsible for organizing events, managing finances, and representing the interests of the members.
Leadership opportunities are available during annual elections. Active participation and commitment are key.
